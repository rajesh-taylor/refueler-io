# B2B Pitch: REFUEL for Fleet Management

## 1. The Reimbursement Problem
- **Context:** Managing home-charging expenses for 2026/27 corporate EV fleets.
- **Friction:** Inaccurate manual reporting and "AER" rate discrepancies.

## 2. The REFUEL Solution: "Digital Notary"
- **Logic:** Certified kWh tracking via BMS-Sync for corporate-to-employee reimbursement.
- **Compliance:** Automated HMRC-compliant reporting to ensure 0% benefit-in-kind (BIK) risk.
- **The "Fleet Switch":** Allow fleet managers to bulk-buy energy credits for employees, settled via the LDK-Plus network.
