# Merchant ROI Dashboard: The "Sats-to-Stout" Analytics

## 1. The Diversion Multiplier (Conquest Logic)
- **Definition:** Measurement of "Net New" customers diverted from a competitor.
- **KPI:** `(Diverted_Users / Total_Regional_Users) * Competitor_Proximity_Weight`.
- **Merchant Value:** Proves Refueler isn't just rewarding existing loyalty; it is actively stealing market share from rival stations within a 1.5-mile radius.

## 2. The "Stout" Index (Basket Correlation)
- **Logic:** Utilizing the **Triple-Verification Dwell Time** to predict in-store retail spend.
- **Thresholds:**
    - **Splash & Dash (< 4 mins):** Fuel only. Low margin for merchant.
    - **The Stout Visit (6 - 10 mins):** Fuel + Convenience Store (e.g., Beer/Stout, Meal Deal). High margin.
    - **The Destination Visit (> 12 mins):** Fuel + Full Grocery Shop. Max margin.
- **Data Goal:** Correlate "Satoshi Path" users with longer dwell times to prove that Bitcoin-rewards attract a higher-spending, tech-savvy demographic.

## 3. The Velocity of Sats (Ecosystem Health)
- **Logic:** Tracking the time between **Payout Ignition** (Exit Vector) and **V4V Activation** (Fountain Stream).
- **KPI:** `Time_to_Stream`.
- **Strategic Advantage:** Shows merchants that Refueler users are "Active" participants in a circular economy, making them 3x more likely to return for the next reward cycle compared to "Passive" fiat users.

## 4. Merchant Dashboard Visuals (ROI View)
- **Heatmap:** Green (High Diversion), Yellow (Organic), Red (Loss of Share).
- **Conversion Funnel:** `Alert Sent` -> `Diversion Tap` -> `3-min Dwell` -> `Verified Sale`.
- **ROI Calculation:** `(Verified_Sales * Avg_Basket_Value) / Refuel_Affiliate_Fee`.
