Pret A Mnager

Pret is about Membership and Habituation. Their entire business model has shifted toward the "Club Pret"subscription. For "Refueler", Pret offers the best lessons in subscription UX and recurring revenue loops.

1. The "Club Pret" Subscription Flow
* The UX Flow: The app’s primary goal is to get you to subscribe. Once a member, the "Home" screen changes to prioritise your Redemption QR Code.
* The "Zero-Decision" Logic: If you’ve already paid for the subscription, the friction of "paying" disappears. You just walk in and scan.
* Refueler Build: You can offer a "Refueler SATS Club." For a monthly fee, users get a SATS-Back Boost (e.g., 500 SATS per coffee instead of 250). It turns a one-off tourist into a recurring "Stacker."

2. The "Digital-First" UX Highlights
* The "Favourite Store" Pivot: Pret doesn't just show a map; it shows your "Home" store’s live menu. If a specific baguette is out of stock at your usual spot, it tells you on the home screen.
* The Apple Watch "Complication": Pret is one of the few that does the Apple Watch well. You can add "Pret QR" as a complication on your watch face. One tap, scan, and you're out.
