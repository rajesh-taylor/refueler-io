1. The Greggs App: "The One-Scan Masterclass"
The Greggs app (v4.32.0) is widely considered the best utility app on the high street. Its success lies in removing every possible micro-barrier to a sale.
* The Single Scan: Unlike other apps where you scan for points and then pay with a card, the Greggs Wallet combines them. One QR code scan handles the loyalty stamp, the payment, and the receipt.
    * Refueler Build: must allow a walker to scan a single QR code that pulls from their SATS/Fiat wallet and confirms the order simultaneously.
* Auto Top-Up: The app encourages users to keep a balance (e.g., £10). When it drops below £5, it auto-refills.
    * Refueler Build: Use this for SATS. Offer an "Auto-Stack" feature where £10 is converted to SATS every time the balance hits zero.

2. The "Bitesize" Format (Your Future Hub Blueprint)
* In 2026, Greggs launched the "Bitesize Greggs" format. Tiny, high-tech units designed for travel hubs where space is at a premium.
* The Logic: Minimum staff, maximum throughput. Relying almost entirely on Click & Collect.
* Refueler Opportunity: Imagine a "Refueler Bitesize Hub" at an EV charging station. No seating, just a high-speed pickup window for people who have 15 minutes left on their charge.

3. The Roadside Strategy (The "EV Gap")
Greggs targets the Roadside & Industrial market, where EV chargers are located.
* Drive-Thrus: They now have over 50 drive-thrus and are opening ~120 new stores this year alone.
* The "Late Night" Trial: They are trialling 24-hour locations at travel hubs.
* Refueler Synergy: EV drivers charging at 2:00 AM are a "forgotten" demographic. If your app shows a 24-hour Greggs is 2 minutes away from the charger, you’ve provided a high-value service that a standard map misses.
