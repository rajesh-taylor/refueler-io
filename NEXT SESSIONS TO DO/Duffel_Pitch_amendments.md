## Duffel Pitch

Pitch email v1.3

Subject: Refueler × Duffel — Partnership Opportunity

I built an eleven-year, concierge-level photography and private walking tour practice serving 100–120 high-net-worth American families per year — guests of Claridge's, The Savoy, and The Connaught who relied on me not just for photographs but for the full London experience: where to eat, which streets to walk, which establishments were worth their time and money. That decade gave me a precise understanding of how internationally mobile, high-disposable-income travellers make decisions — and critically, how they plan: high-value travel bookings in this market are made 8 to 12 weeks in advance, anchored to school vacation windows and major calendar events. Refueler is built around that planning behaviour.

The immediate opportunity is a specific and underserved traveller: internationally mobile Bitcoiners with confirmed event tickets, multi-city itineraries, and no single platform that handles their inter-city travel, destination planning, and a rewards layer that makes every booking financially meaningful. Refueler, with Duffel's flights and car hire API at its core, is that platform. The upcoming World Cup is an honest entry point and proof of concept — not the ceiling. The international sporting and touring calendar is permanent, Euro 2028 is UK-hosted, and the school holiday market follows cyclical patterns that suit a distribution partnership designed for the long term.

I have been active in the Bitcoin ecosystem since 2021 — attending conferences, regular meetups including Cypherpunk House London and the Essex and Battersea Bitcoin community — and I understand this audience from the inside. They are financially engaged, digitally native, and they travel internationally with intent. They are not a niche. They are an early signal of how the next generation of high-value travellers will book, spend, and expect to be rewarded.

Rajesh Taylor
