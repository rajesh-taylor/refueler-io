## The "Triple-Win" Economic Loop
- **Phase 1 (The Trigger):** US traveler picks up a Duffel-booked rental; REFUEL activates the "Traveler Hub" geofence.
- **Phase 2 (The Save):** REFUEL diverts the driver to a verified partner (e.g., Sainsbury's), saving **15p/Litre (£7.50/tank)**.
- **Phase 3 (The Reward):** A verified "Proof-of-Refuel" triggers a **500 Satoshi** LDK reward.
- **Phase 4 (The Support):** User "zaps" those Sats to a Fountain creator via the CarPlay dashboard.
- **Phase 5 (Sustainability):** The fuel savings effectively cover the cost of the content, creating a **Frictionless Circular Economy**.

A critical distinction. To reach mass-market adoption, REFUEL must be a "Savings App" first and a "Bitcoin App" second. The Bitcoin element should feel like a "Premium Upgrade" or a "Digital Bonus," while Fiat remains the comfortable baseline for the average traveler.

## Pricing Psychology (Sats vs Bits)
- **Rule:** Always display rewards in **Satoshis** (e.g., "1,500 Sats") to maximize perceived weight. 
- **Comparison:** Avoid "0.000015 BTC," as decimals minimize reward satisfaction.
