REFUELER
Value proposition · v1.1
May 2026 · Pre-TestFlight
The platform that knows you're coming.
Order ahead on the train. Collect at the platform. Earn real Bitcoin with every purchase — no card, no queue, no waiting. Built for London commuters. Powered by Lightning.
For the commuter
Your flat white is ready when you arrive.
Refueler detects your train is moving and asks if you want your usual. By the time you reach Fenchurch Street, it's waiting. No app-opening, no queue, no wasted minutes at the counter.
  • Under 2 minutes at the counter — vs. 8–12 in a queue
  • Every purchase earns sats. Real Bitcoin. Not points.
  • Your location never leaves your phone. The app works it out locally.
  • New to Bitcoin? Start with loyalty stamps. Switch to sats when ready.
  • Someone else collecting? Share a link. No account needed.
For the merchant
More orders. Faster throughput. A new kind of customer.
Refueler sends confirmed, pre-paid orders directly to your Command Centre tablet. Staff prepare on time. The customer arrives, collects, and leaves. Your queue moves faster. And you gain access to a customer your loyalty card never reached.
  • Pre-paid orders only. No no-shows, no cancellations.
  • Loyalty cost co-funded via sats subsidy — not your margin.
  • Command Centre is a clean tablet UI. Minimal training required.
  • No consumer data passes through you. GDPR liability stays with us.
  • Walk-in and walk-past orders captured — not just pre-orders.
  • New foot flow: Bitcoin stackers. A high-spending, loyalty-averse demographic concentrated in the City of London — and they tip in sats.
For the investor
A new payment rail hiding inside a coffee app.
Refueler is a Bitcoin Lightning payments platform dressed as a commuter convenience product. The addressable market is every daily-spend transaction in London's transport corridors — starting with 60,000+ daily C2C passengers and expanding across EV networks, stadium concessions, and travel booking.
  • Dual revenue: transaction fee per order + SaaS per venue
  • Moat: on-device geofence UX is not replicable by ad-tech loyalty apps
  • Privacy architecture as competitive differentiator — zero location data
  • Bitcoin-native rewards = no interchange, no points liability
  • London fintech context: adjacent to Monzo, Revolut — but on a parallel rail
