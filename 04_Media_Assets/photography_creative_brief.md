# Photography & Visual Creative Brief: "The Cockpit Aesthetic"

## 1. Hero Product Shots (The "Hero" Watch)
- **Style:** High-end horology (Tag Heuer/Breguet inspiration).
- **Lighting:** Moody, directional lighting with deep shadows.
- **Key Visual:** Focus on the mechanical complexity—specifically the Roman numeral serif and the #F7931A Orange "Sats Switch" toggle.

## 2. In-Situ "Cockpit" Views (CarPlay & HUD)
- **Environment:** Premium automotive interiors (Mercedes/Audi/Range Rover).
- **Composition:** Shallow depth-of-field, focusing on the CarPlay screen or the iPhone mounted on the dash.
- **Lighting:** Night/Dusk "Cityscape" lighting (Blues and ambers) to complement the Slate Blue/Cream palette.

## 3. The "Open Road" Narrative
- **Visuals:** Wide-angle shots of the car on scenic routes (e.g., Scottish Highlands/A82).
- **Goal:** Link the high-utility digital reward to the physical freedom of travel.
