Refueler is a small team building something we care about. If we've fallen short — a missed order, a reward that didn't land, something that felt off — we want to know. Every complaint is read by a person, not a ticket system. Please contact us, we are here to help you.

How it works

Tell us. Send us a message at privacy@refueler.io with a short description of what happened. You don't need to include order numbers or account details upfront — just tell us in your own words.

We respond. We'll acknowledge your message within 24 hours. A member of the team — not a bot — will read what you've sent and come back to you with a proper response within 30 days. For most concerns, it's much quicker than that.

We make it right. If we've made a mistake, we'll fix it. That might mean reissuing a reward, correcting your account, or simply explaining what happened. We'll tell you what we've done and why.

What can I raise?

A reward that wasn't issued or was the wrong amount

An order that wasn't ready or wasn't right

A question about your personal data or how we've used it

Anything else that felt wrong — we'd rather hear it
