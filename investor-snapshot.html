Refueler is a Bitcoin-native pre-order platform for commuters on the Fenchurch St line (Shoeburyness → Fenchurch Street). Commuters place orders before they arrive; merchants fulfil at the counter. Payment settles via the Lightning Network — no custody, non-reversible, instant. The loyalty stamp bridge onboards non-Bitcoin users and nudges them toward sats. Merchant integration is web-based, requiring no additional hardware.
